safety.viz hep waterfall demo

Demo and Playwright harness page for the hep-waterfall module (#93) — the modified ALT waterfall of Amirzadegan et al., Drug Safety 2025;48(5):443-453, Figure 5. Loads the committed IIFE bundle against a small crafted cohort engineered so every case the figure exists to show is present exactly once and can be asserted by value: a bar that drops below the baseline trace in each arm, a bar that rises above it, a new-onset-jaundice bar in each arm (so the green-over-arm precedence is provable rather than assumed), a participant excluded by the paper's Table-1 baseline-bilirubin rule, a participant excluded because their arm is designated neither placebo nor active, and two records dropped for a missing reference range.
